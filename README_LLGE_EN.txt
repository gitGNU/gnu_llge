Libre library of graphic elements
Libre library of graphic elements (LLGE) is a free library of graphical elements in the format *.dxf, *.png, *.svg, *.odg designed for GNU GPL software LibreCAD, DIA, Libre Office, Inkscape, etc.
LLGE contains graphic symbols of electronic circuits, block diagrams, electrical systems, relay protection and primary equipment of the substations and hatch patterns that comply with national standards such as GOST, ISO, etc.
